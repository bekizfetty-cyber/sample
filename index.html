About ADO Bar And Restaurant

ADO Bar And Restaurant offers a lively bar atmosphere and local hospitality in Addis Ababa. The venue is known locally as a neighborhood bar/restaurant and is centrally located in the Kirkos area, making it an accessible spot for both residents and visitors to enjoy drinks, casual dining, and social evenings.
